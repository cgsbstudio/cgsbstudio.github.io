Stewart Bird is a creative director, designer, and artist who specializes in technical multimedia production, exploring the intersection between 3D production, animation, web based interactivity, audio, exhibition design, and game development. After receiving a Masters in Fine Arts from Pratt Institute, Stewart joined the Hong Kong-based design firm, ioio, where he specialized in 3D modeling, multimedia, and exhibition design. Stewart began his career producing and playing music, while also building 8-Channel immersive sound installations internationally with prominent Fluxus composer Charlie Morrow (MorrowSound). He has been featured in Resident Advisor, Fact Magazine, Dummy Magazine, Spin Magazine, and his media appearances include NTS, Boiler Room, The Lot Radio, BBC Radio 1 and 1Xtra. He received a Bachelors in Emergent Digital Practices with an emphasis on Max MSP from the University of Denver, and attended the Red Bull Music Academy: Studio Science in New York and the Institut For (x) residency in Aarhus, Denmark for audio composition. He believes creative potency exists on the margins between multiple toolsets, and the ability to complete production work from end to end gives his output a strong sense of cohesion and character.

Chris Gouchoe is a producer, narrative strategist, and writer, who has spent the last 15 years working across film, video games, commercials, theater, radio, and new media. As the first employee and Head of Marketing at KitBash3D, he worked with Disney, HBO, Epic Games, Unity, and thousands of global indie artists to help create movies, video games, and interactive media. He began in Entertainment as an actor, appearing in films and national commercials (The Social Network, Game of Thrones “Own the Throne” campaign), and served as a producer, writer, and voice artist on the Nintendo Switch game Sleep Tight. He also recently completed his first novel and helps run the Young Publishers Association in NYC. Chris is drawn to the crossroads of timeless storytelling principles and emerging media tech, always on the hunt for ways to enhance, break, or reimagine conventional narrative forms.
