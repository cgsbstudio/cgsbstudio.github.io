With over two decades of musical training in jazz and contemporary composition, Stewart Bird works in interactive audio/visual software, music production, and new media. He spent the majority of his twenties building 8-Channel immersive sound installations internationally with avant garde composer, Charlie Morrow, and his production company, MorrowSound. Stewart attended the Red Bull Music Academy: Studio Science in New York and the Institut For (x) residency in Aarhus, Denmark for audio composition. He has been featured in Resident Advisor, Fact Magazine, Dummy Magazine, Spin Magazine, and his media appearances include NTS, The Lot Radio, BBC Radio 1 and 1Xtra. He has a Masters in Fine Arts from Pratt Institute and a Bachelors in Emergent Digital Practices with an emphasis on Max MSP and Audio Installation from the University of Denver.
